Where I am from...

My hometown is Nanchang City in Jiangxi Province of China, but many people do not know the place so I tell them I came from Shanghai since my dad works here.

My parents are both from Jiangxi Nanchang. However, since my dad works in Shanghai, and he has been working here for more than 20 years, sometimes I say I came from Shanghai instead of Nanchang.

I have an older brother Daniel who is four years older than me. His name is Daniel, or Minghao Deng.

ALthough I only have one brother, I have many cousins, and most of them from my dad's side are older than me. But the ones from my mom's side are all boys and younger than me.

I also have a dog, and her name is Mifan which translates to "rice" in Chinese. We adopted her last summer because she was dumped by her owner when she was a month old. She is eight months old now.
